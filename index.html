About Us

Meldgies Rivers Edge Cafe is a must-visit restaurant in Wakefield, Rhode Island, where we serve up delicious and fresh breakfast and lunch dishes using locally-sourced ingredients from our trusted vendors. Whether you choose to dine in or take out, our menu features a range of classic and homestyle meals, such as turkey dinner, fish and chips, meatloaf, and hot weiners. We are open daily from early morning until afternoon and offer daily breakfast and lunch specials. Enjoy our warm and welcoming atmosphere while indulging in our delicious coffee selection, including flavored, decaf, caffinated, hot and iced options. Come visit us at Meldgies Rivers Edge Cafe, where everyone is treated like family, and experience the taste of fresh, locally sourced dishes.
